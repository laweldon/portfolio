About the Breed

Pitbulls come in many colors

From "blue" to "fawn" and many more, pitbulls can be many different colors. They also range in weight, height, and overall build.

Pitbulls are not one breed

What many call "pitbulls" in fact includes a wide range of breeds, from Staffordshire Terriers to English Bull Terriers and every mutt in between!
